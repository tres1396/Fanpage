Beyonce Giselle Knowles-Carter

Biography

Beyonce is a singer, record producer, actress, business woman, fashion icon, and philanthropist. Born in Houston, Texas, she began singing at a young age and eventually broke into the music scene in 1997.
She sang with the highly successful group 'Destiny's Child' and broke off into her solo career in which she has seen enormous success. Some of her most popular songs include "Single Ladies", "Crazy In Love", and "Halo".
